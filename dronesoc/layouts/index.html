What is DroneSoc?

DroneSoc is a society for people interested in flying or building drones! Everyone is welcome, whether you're a seasoned expert or a complete beginner.

As a society we'll mostly be focusing on quadcopters, but we're open for drones of all kinds to get involved in DroneSoc.
